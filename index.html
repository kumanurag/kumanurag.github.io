Molecular Imprinted Polymer

Fabricated a Benzopyrene imprinted polyurethane polymer film to detect and remove benzopyrene at very low cconcentrations. Cometration as low as 0.4pM of benzopyrene could be detected with 100% efficieny suggesting the possibility of detection of even lower conctration. The effect of factors such as elutant concentration, film thickness, etc. on the the efficiency and selectivity of the sensor were studied. Obtained an efficiency of more than 50% for the removal of benzopyrene at pico-molar level

Enthesis regeneration

Hyphothesized that Smad3 expression happens in a gradient fashion across ligament-to-bone interface with the highest expression at soft tissue side and lowest expression at bone tissue side and this gradient could be regulated by microRNAs. A gradient of miRNAs can bewas generated in a stem cell seeded collagen scaffold using poly(L-Lysine). Various immunohistochemical and characterization experiments to validate the hpyothesis and speculations were suggested.

Electroactive Co-Polymer Scaffold

Proposed an electrically conductive polymer composite consisting of Collagen type-1 crosslinked with Chondroitin sulphate doped polypyrrole that could be electrochemically deposited with hydroxyapatite as an scaffold for bone regeneration.

Electrochemical approach to mineralize the scaffold isn't just a rapid process but also can help control the thickness of the deposition.
